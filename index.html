03 About Me

Code that's clean. Design that's sharp.

Hi, I’m Praveen Pramodh — an undergraduate at SLIIT and a passionate web developer who enjoys turning ideas into interactive, meaningful digital experiences.

I combine strong frontend development skills with a keen eye for design, focusing on building websites that are not only functional and efficient but also visually engaging and user-friendly. I enjoy solving real-world problems through clean code and thoughtful UI/UX design.

My goal is to continuously push beyond basic templates and “standard” design patterns, creating web experiences that feel modern, creative, and impactful. I’m especially interested in building scalable web applications and improving software quality through good development practices.

As I continue my journey in software engineering, I’m always exploring new technologies, refining my skills, and working on projects that challenge me to grow as a developer.
